01

Everything you copy, kept.

The moment you press ⌘C, LumaClip silently captures the content, classifies it, and adds it to your local history. Text, URLs, code, emails, file paths, hex colors, phone numbers, and images — each detected automatically and tagged.

✓
Auto-classification — content types recognized in milliseconds, with color-coded icons in the list.
✓
Local-only by default — your clipboard history is stored in a sandboxed SQLite database. Nothing ever leaves your Mac.
✓
Privacy-aware — sensitive apps (1Password, Keychain) and copies marked confidential are skipped automatically.

02

Master the keyboard.

LumaClip is built for fingers, not mice. Finder-style column navigation lets you sweep across the sidebar, list, and inspector without lifting your hands. ⌃⇧V opens the Quick Paste panel from anywhere in macOS.

✓
Three-column focus model — left/right arrows shift focus between columns, up/down move within.
✓
Quick Paste panel — a Spotlight-like floating panel that pastes the selected clip into the previously-focused app.
✓
Global hotkey — fully customizable. Set it once in Settings; works everywhere.

03

Bundles for repeat workflows.

Group related clips into named Bundles, then paste them sequentially with a single keystroke. Perfect for filling forms, drafting emails from templates, or pasting boilerplate code blocks in order.

✓
Sequential paste mode — each press of the Bundle hotkey advances to the next item, with a floating HUD showing your progress.
✓
Drag-to-reorder — rearrange items inside a bundle with smooth spring animations.
✓
Categories & favorites — alongside bundles, build your own categorization rules and pin clips you reach for daily.

Full-text indexed — even tens of thousands of clips return results instantly.
✓
Smart suggestions — hit Tab to autocomplete tokens as you type.
